3 Great Amusement Parks in the Luzon

Enchanted Kingdom

  • Many different rides and attractions
  • Lines can be long, but the rides are high quality
  • A few themed areas
  • A good amount of amenities

Enchanted Kingdom is an amusement Park filled with many different rides and attractions that will keep the whole family entertained. While the lines on some of the rides can be rather long, the wait is usually worth it, as all of the rides are of high quality.


Splash Island

  • Many thrilling rides and slides
  • More relaxing attractions such as the lazy river and minigolf
  • Option to stay overnight with many different accomodations
  • Good amenities, but the shower area seems rather old and dirty

Splash island is a water park filled with many fun rides and events, including a wave pool, water slides, etc. There are also some considerably more relaxing options available for those who just want to unwind, such as the lazy river or minigolf.

Sky Ranch

  • Thrilling rides
  • Cold wind and view of Tagaytay
  • Attractions for both children and adults

Admittedly, I haven't been here myself, however the reviews that I have read, and my time in Tagaytay make this place sound like a blast.
